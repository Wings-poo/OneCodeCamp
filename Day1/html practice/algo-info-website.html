UP Algorithm Plus-Plus

A Community for Competitive Programming Enthusiasts

Vision

UP Algorithm Plus-Plus shall develop students who are competent at problem solving in the form of programming regardless of the application being for competitions or for real-life applications.

The organization also envisions itself to represent the university in both local and international programming competitions.

The organization advocates competitive programming in the university; not just for the purpose of competing internationally, but for the purpose of learning from the ideas behind the sport.

Mission

UP Algorithm Plus-Plus dedicates itself to provide an avenue for students in UP Diliman who are interested in competitive programming, both for recreational purposes and competitive purposes.

It aims to develop its members to be skilled in competitive programming by means of training and lectures. With the formation of the members, it aims to compete in several competitions bearing the name of the university.
